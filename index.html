Assessment of the Delta Sector

Report Summary

This report is the second of two produced during this assessment where we identify and assess the technology landscape, highlighting the service relationship, topology and health.

It provides colour and substance on such subjects as the profile of operational sites, interrelationships and dependencies, the state of technology health and the drivers of service risk across our technology network. It documents the full scope of the operating network within the Delta sector, from detailed analyses of the +40 operating sites across all technology areas. This report aims to create transparency to Operational and Project Teams to enable evidence-based investment decisions as well as accelerate resolution of faults.
